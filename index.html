My APA is a team of academics and professionals across different fields and countries. My APA is committed to Excellence, striving to deliver high-quality services and experiences that support the professional development of academics and professionals globally. Our dedication to Diversity and Inclusion means fostering an environment that welcomes individuals from varied backgrounds and perspectives. Through Collaboration, we work closely with clients, universities, and other stakeholders to create tailored solutions and opportunities. We uphold Integrity by conducting our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices. By embracing Continuous Learning, we stay ahead in an ever-evolving field, fostering lifelong learning and innovation. Our focus on Community Impact ensures we empower academics to make positive contributions to their fields and communities.

Our mission includes (1) Empowering Academic Growth, Support professional development and advancement with tailored training programs and opportunities; (2) Facilitating International Collaboration, Foster global partnerships and knowledge exchange between international academics and UK institutions. (3) Enabling Career Mobility, Provide resources and support for skill expansion, enhancing career prospects and global mobility for academics and professionals. (4) Promoting Cultural Exchange, Create enriching experiences that bridge cultural gaps, fostering understanding and collaboration among diverse communities. (5) Driving Academic and Professional Excellence, Elevate academic research and practice standards by equipping scholars with the tools and knowledge to excel.

To become (1) a Well-established Provider of Academic and Professional Development Services: MyAPA aims to be the foremost provider of academic and professional development services for foreign scholars and professionals seeking opportunities in the UK. ,(2) Supportive and Inclusive Environment: We strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment that empowers academics and professionals to achieve their career and personal goals. ,(3) Facilitating Connections and Collaborations: Our goal is to facilitate meaningful connections and collaborations between international scholars and UK institutions, promoting knowledge exchange and innovation. ,(4) Advancement of Academic Excellence: We are committed to contributing to the advancement of academic excellence and research standards through tailored training programmes and resources. and (5) Trusted Partner for Universities and Organisations: MyAPA seeks to establish itself as a trusted partner for universities and academic institutions in meeting the needs of international scholars.

Services

Academic and Professional Development Programs

My APA offers a range of tailored training programs designed to enhance the skills and expertise of academics and professionals. These programs are crafted to meet the specific needs of individuals looking to advance their careers in various fields, including social sciences, education, computing, engineering, STEM, and medical subjects. Our development programs include workshops, seminars, and internships that are conducted in collaboration with prestigious UK universities, ensuring that participants receive high-quality, relevant, and up-to-date education.

International Collaboration
and Exchange
Services

My APA facilitates international collaboration by fostering connections between academics and professionals from around the world and UK institutions. We provide services that promote cultural exchange and knowledge transfer, such as study abroad opportunities, joint research projects, and international conferences. Our goal is to bridge cultural gaps and encourage understanding and collaboration among diverse academic and professional communities.

Career Mobility
and Networking
Support Services


My APA supports the career progression of academics and professionals by providing resources and opportunities that enhance their global mobility. We offer services that help individuals expand their skills, qualifications, and networks, including career counseling, job placement assistance, internships, apprenticeships and networking events. Our extensive network of partners and clients ensures that we can connect our members with the right opportunities to further their careers, both in the UK and internationally.
